Are you Creative?

 

Lets have a test .. This is a test for all those who think they are creative. We give you restricted resources to build your thoughts.
This is a test for you to build your imagination. Draw!!! Scribble!!! Paint and show your creativity and creation. Put up anything your mind can think of, Something you think stands outstanding among the others. The names of the best creations will be put up on this website soon by the end of April. Dont forget to share your creation on facebook and tag your friends.

You may need to know this before you begin
Position an element by dragging
Adjust it by moving its control points
Delete it by using the delete key on your keyboard
'F'  ----  brings an element to Front
'U' ----  increases(up) the opacity of an element
'L' decreases(down) the opacity of an element
Color the elements by using the color panel at the top right corner. You may change background color too by clicking the background.
